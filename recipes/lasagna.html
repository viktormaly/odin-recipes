Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Marinara sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Onion (diced)
  • Olive oil
  • Basil (fresh or dried)
  • Oregano (dried)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Egg (for mixing with ricotta)
  • Spinach (optional)
  • Mushrooms (sliced, optional)
  • Zucchini (sliced, optional)
  • Carrot (finely diced, optional)
  • Celery (finely diced, optional)
  • Red wine (optional, for deglazing)
  • Bay leaf (optional, for sauce)

How to create a Mama Mia Lasagna

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the ground beef or Italian sausage to the skillet. Cook until browned, breaking it up with a spatula as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce and marinara sauce (or crushed tomatoes). Season with salt, black pepper, basil, and oregano. Add red pepper flakes if using. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld. If using red wine, add it now to deglaze the pan and let it reduce slightly.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and fresh parsley. Mix well until smooth. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  7. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat the layering process: add another layer of sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Continue until you’ve used up all the ingredients, finishing with a layer of sauce and a generous sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, making sure it's tented slightly to avoid touching the cheese. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese is golden and slightly browned.
  12. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before serving. This will help it set and make it easier to slice.
  13. Garnish with additional fresh parsley or basil, if desired. Serve warm and enjoy!
